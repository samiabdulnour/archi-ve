Common questions

Where are my photos stored?

On your device, and — if you’re signed in to iCloud — in your own private iCloud account, so they sync across your devices. The developer can’t see them. See the privacy policy.

Do I need an account?

No. There’s no sign-in. Syncing uses your iCloud automatically.

Will my photos sync to my other devices?

Yes, as long as you’re signed in to the same iCloud account and iCloud Drive is on. Sync happens in the background.

Can I back up or export?

Yes — you can export a full backup to the Files app, and every shot can be saved to your Photos library.

How do I delete my data?

Delete any photo (or all of them) in the app. Deleting the app removes its on-device data; iCloud-synced data can be removed from your iCloud settings.

About archi-ve

ARCHI-ve.

by Sami Abdulnour

A private photo journal for an architect. Take photos as you walk — site visits, references spotted in the world, exhibitions, details — tag them in two taps, find them later.

  1. 1
    Type
    Three icons in one pill — Building, Element, Graphic. Tap to set what you're capturing; the active icon highlights.
  2. 2
    Tag · Reuse · More
    The right pill holds three actions. The tag icon toggles Lite ↔ Full (Lite saves now, Full opens the tagger after the shutter). ↻ reuses the tags from your last reference photo. ⋯ opens the settings sheet (grid, level, flash, vocabularies, theme).
  3. 3
    Shutter
    Tap to capture. In Reference Lite, a green toast lets you tag now or in the gallery later.
  4. 4
    Gallery
    Latest photo as a circular thumbnail. Tap to browse, search, filter, or share/delete in batches.
  5. 5
    Reference vs Project
    Reference for photos found out there. Project for site-specific shoots — pick the project once, shoot a sequence into it.
  6. 6
    Flip camera
    Switch between rear and front cameras.

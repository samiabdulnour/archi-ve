About archi-archive

archi-archive

A private photo journal for an architect. Take photos as you walk — site visits, references spotted in the world, exhibitions, details — tag them in two taps, find them later.

  1. 1
    Type & Reuse
    Pick what you're capturing — Building, Detail, or Graphic. The ↻ button reuses the tags from your last reference photo for fast repeats.
  2. 2
    Lite vs Full · Settings
    L saves now, tag later. F opens the tagger right after the shutter. The ⋯ icon opens settings.
  3. 3
    Shutter
    Tap to capture. In Reference Lite, a green toast lets you tag now or in the gallery later.
  4. 4
    Gallery
    Latest photo as a circular thumbnail. Tap to browse, search, filter, or share/delete in batches.
  5. 5
    Reference vs Project
    Reference for found photos out there. Project for site-specific shoots — pick the project once, shoot a sequence into it.
  6. 6
    Flip camera
    Switch between rear and front cameras.
